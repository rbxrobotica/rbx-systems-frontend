description: Editorial rules for RBX site copy - avoid agency speak, prefer engineering language

When writing or editing copy for the RBX site, follow Zurich-coded style: less adjective more architecture, less creativity more precision, less marketing more system, less agency more engineering.

**Why:** The founder explicitly rejected agency-style copy ("transformamos ideias em realidade", "solucoes inovadoras", "presenca digital") and wants the site to reflect an engineering firm that builds systems infrastructure.

**How to apply:** Every text on the site should pass the test: "Does this sound like it could come from a Swiss B2B engineering firm?" If not, rewrite with sober, structural, institutional language. Do NOT touch Atelier or Produtos pages.
